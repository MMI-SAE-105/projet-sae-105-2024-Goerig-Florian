About Me

Hello, my name is Florian Goerig, I am 34 years old, and I am currently in the process of career change. I went back to school to join the BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) program at the University of Montbéliard.

Project Topic

The topic is to create a mobile-first website with the mandatory theme of the prehistory of the future.

I decided to create a futuristic science fiction website that immerses today’s readers in articles that could have been written by journalists in 2500.

These articles revolve around the impact of oil platforms on society and the environment.

If you have any questions or recommendations, feel free to contact me!
