Glossary

Find clear and concise definitions of essential terms to better understand our universe here.


Prophetic

Referring to the ability to predict the future accurately, often associated with warnings or visions of the future.

Indictment

A speech or text that accuses and sharply criticizes a person, idea, or situation.

Energy Dependence

The critical need for a society to rely on specific energy sources, such as oil.

Ruins

What remains of a bygone past, often used in an archaeological context to refer to ruins or ancient objects.

Collapse

The sudden and dramatic fall of a system or structure, whether social, economic, or environmental.

Media Manipulation

A strategy aimed at influencing public opinion through the media, often for misinformation or control purposes.
