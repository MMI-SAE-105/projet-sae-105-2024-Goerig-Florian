Timeline

1891:
First freshwater offshore platform (Lake St. Mary’s, Ohio).

1930:
First shallow-water platforms in the Gulf of Mexico.

1947:
First offshore platform out of sight of land (Gulf of Mexico).

1960:
Development of fixed platforms for shallow waters.

1980:
Emergence of TLP (Tension Leg Platforms).

2000:
Innovations for ultra-deepwater exploitation.
